Violin Performances

Here is a collection of performances I have participated in including solos, duets, works with the University of Washington Symphony Orchestra, Inglemoor High School Chamber Orchestra, and the Youth Symphony Orchestra in the Seattle Youth Symphony Orchestras.

UWSO Concert (2025)

I played in the first violins seated at fifth chair! The concert was in Meany Hall. This is one of my favorite performances!

Senior Recital (2023)

I performed a duet for my violin studio's summer recital as my last performance during high school!

Youth Symphony Orchestra Concert (2022)

I played in 5th chair of the first violins during this Seattle Youth Symphony Orchestras concert at Benaroya Hall.

Chamber Orchestra Washington State Competition (2023)

I was the concertmaster of my high school's chamber orchestra and we got 3rd place at the state competition.

Youth Symphony Orchestra Concert (2023)

I played in 8th chair of the first violins during this Seattle Youth Symphony Orchestras concert at Benaroya Hall. Enjoy the accompanying light show!

UWSO Concert (2024)

I played in 7th chair of the first violins during this University of Washington Symphony Orchestra concert at Meany Hall.

Virtuoso Festival (2022)

I received a gold medal in the Virtuoso Festival in the Concerto: Ages 14 to 18 division.
